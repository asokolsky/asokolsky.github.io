## Google Fonts Dispenser

Try fonts [here](https://fonts.google.com).  Once the you happy with your collection:

* download the zip
* unzip it to ~/.fonts:
```sh
unzip Source_Code_Pro.zip  -d ~/.fonts/Source_Code_Pro
```
* refresh font cache
```sh
fc-cache -f
```
