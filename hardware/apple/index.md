# Apple

## AppleTV 4K

### Hidden Menu

[..has A Hidden Menu](https://www.slashgear.com/2105346/apple-tv-hidden-menu-how-to-access/)

1. Turn on your AppleTV box using its remote control.
2. With your AppleTV remote control in hand, navigate to the Settings and select it.
3. Scroll down to the System tab and click into it.
4. In the System menu, navigate to and select the Software Updates tab.
5. Once in that menu, press the Play/Pause button four times in rapid succession.

You will be immediately routed to the hidden Internal Settings menu, with tabs for

* AppleConnect
* VPN
* Configuration

### More Gestures

#### TV/Control Center button (little TV icon)

* double-click - view open apps and switch between them on the fly. Once you settle on the app, press up twice on the circular clickpad to close it.  Alternatively swipe up on the internal trackpad inside the circle on newer remotes.
* press and hold - pop-up Control Center menu, allowing you to switch between users, adjust audio settings, set the sleep timer, or power off the Apple TV
* To Restart/Unfreeze: press and hold, simultaneously press and hold the Volume Down button for about
5 seconds until the Apple TV status light turns off and on again.
