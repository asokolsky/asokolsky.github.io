Action|Key Sequence
------|------------
Switch OS Mac/Win|Fn + Caps
.|Lighting
Change lighting effect|Fn + Q
Turn backlight on/off|Fn + Tab
Increase backlight brightness|Fn + W
Decrease backlight brightness|Fn + S
Factory reset|Fn + J + Z (for 4 seconds).
